# Attribution & Image Rights

This repository includes local emblem images in `assets/symbols/`.

Important: Some emblems (national symbols, organisational emblems, UN insignia, etc.) may be subject to legal restrictions or third-party rights. Contributors and downstream users must ensure they have the right to redistribute, publish, or reuse any image.

If you add or change images, include a short attribution line here with the source and any license information.

Example attribution entry:

- `assets/symbols/red_cross.png` — Provided by [source], licensed under [license].
