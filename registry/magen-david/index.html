Magen David Adom (Red Star of David)

Contents

  1. Historical origin
  2. Legal basis and misuse
  3. References

Historical origin

Magen David Adom developed as the national emblem of Israel's emergency medical and humanitarian organisation. Its use reflects national identity while international arrangements enabled its operational participation through the Red Crystal framework to reconcile national emblems with international protective signalling.

Legal context and misuse

Magen David Adom holds national protection in Israel and participates internationally via arrangements compatible with the Red Crystal mechanism to ensure protective signalling in multinational operations. Unauthorized or decorative use that implies official humanitarian authority is restricted and may be subject to national sanctions.
